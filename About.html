About TAP

The TAP program aims to offer a more tech focused education experience for students who are interested in the tech sector.

Students get the opportunity to be prepared for career opportunies in the technology sector with mentorships, co-ops, internships, placements in NSCC, and a garenteed interview with IBM at the end.

The following schools are involved with the TAP program;

  • Cole Harbour District High School
  • JL Ilsley High School
  • Yarmouth Consolidated Memorial High School
